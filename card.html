Capture the Flag (CTF)
Ahmad Ray Septa Firdaus | Hobbies

Capture the Flag (CTF) in cyber security is an exercise in which "flags" are secretly hidden in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites. Competitors steal flags either from other competitors (attack/defense-style CTFs) or from the organizers (jeopardy-style challenges).
